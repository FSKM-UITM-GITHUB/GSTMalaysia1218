Dataset:		1. Original Dataset.csv

Description: 		Contains the original extracted Twitter data from 14 states in Malaysia that were merged into one file. 
			The dataset consists of 77,637 tweets from 11,628 users.


Dataset: 		2. Preprocessed Dataset.csv

Description: 		Contains Twitter data that has gone through a series of preprocessing steps; 
			Assigned state variable, removed non-Malay tweets, removed tweets with links and removed spams & duplicates. 
			The dataset consists of 8,829 tweets from 5,016 users.
